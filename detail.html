Vegetarian Tacos

4.3 stars

Ingredients

Amount Food item
1 cup Green beans
3 ears Fresh Corn
2 cups Tomatillo salsa
8 Corn tortillas
0.5 Cotija cheese

Steps

  1. Blanch the green beans
  2. Grill the green beans and the ears of corn
  3. Chop the green beans
  4. Grill the tortillas
  5. Assemble the tacos with veggies, salsa, and cheese
